# Claim Boundaries

AOI must use bounded, inspectable language.

## Allowed Claims

- AOI is a read-only MCP/API objective ranking tool.
- AOI helps compare AI tools, APIs, SaaS products, and MCP servers against explicit objectives and constraints.
- Extracted or sample data includes source traces.
- AOI v0.1 can act as a benchmark and data-contract tool.
- AOI can surface missing fields, warnings, score components, and decision receipts.

## Forbidden Claims

- All AI will use AOI first.
- AOI is an official standard.
- AOI guarantees product quality.
- AOI provides legal, financial, medical, purchasing, procurement, compliance, or professional advice.
- AOI automatically purchases, books, pays, logs in, sends email, submits forms, or signs contracts.
- AOI rankings are paid placement unless a future product explicitly and transparently creates a separate sponsored surface.

## Required Disclaimer

AOI output is not a quality guarantee and is not legal, financial, medical, purchasing, procurement, compliance, or professional advice. Important decisions require primary-source review and human or organization-specific approval.

## Research Claim Ceiling Is Not Productization Prohibition

Internal research source status does not automatically prove product readiness, field validation, security validation, legal readiness, or external theorem settlement.

That claim ceiling is not a ban on building software. AOI may productize research-inspired concepts into schemas, algorithms, benchmark reports, MCP/API prototypes, GitHub repos, demos, and commercialization experiments.

The boundary is public assertion: product claims require product evidence. Research inspiration can justify implementation, but it cannot by itself justify claims of readiness, certification, legal sufficiency, security validation, official standard status, purchasing advice, or action authority.

## Package 6D-S Source-Governed Boundary

Package 6D-S adds a source-governed claim ceiling before promotion. Generated, sample, stale, or unverified records cannot be silently promoted to verified supplier claims.

AOI may recommend an option for a read-only comparison, but recommendation is not an action license. Buying, booking, paying, logging in, emailing, submitting forms, connecting accounts, purchasing, and signing contracts remain blocked.

HOLD and BLOCK outputs are productive non-closures: they should name the missing evidence and the next review step instead of pretending the decision is fully closed.
