# AgentSec Pilot Claim Boundaries

An AgentSec pilot receipt may say:

- local/offline manifest review
- ALLOW/HOLD/BLOCK findings
- permission scope indicators
- hidden instruction indicators
- namespace review signals
- unsupported claim findings
- forbidden action findings
- known limits and next actions

It must not claim:

- security certification
- compliance audit
- quality guarantee
- tool safety guarantee
- production readiness
- live exploit scan
- verification status
- permission to perform outside actions
- legal sufficiency

Private kernel details, ranking calibration values, thresholds, provider priors, anti-gaming policy details, private negative controls, private probe seeds, and commercial routing policy remain non-public.
