# DataCapsule Pilot Claim Boundaries

A DataCapsule pilot receipt may say:

- local/offline manifest review artifact
- ALLOW/HOLD/BLOCK findings
- source and rights metadata gaps
- privacy/PII manifest gaps
- evaluation-boundary gaps
- use-boundary decisions
- known limits and next actions

It must not claim:

- legal opinion
- privacy audit
- license clearance
- evaluation-cleanliness proof
- data quality guarantee
- training authorization
- action authorization
- product readiness

Private kernel details, scoring calibration values, thresholds, provider priors, anti-gaming policy details, private negative controls, private probe seeds, and commercial routing policy remain non-public.
