# External Share Claim Boundaries

The external-safe share pack is not:

- no external pilot;
- no product launch;
- no security certification;
- no code correctness proof;
- no legal opinion;
- no privacy audit;
- no license clearance;
- no eval-clean proof;
- no quality guarantee;
- no product-readiness claim;
- no external action authorization.

The pack may describe a static local demo, local receipt artifacts, ALLOW/HOLD/BLOCK summaries, feedback memory, known limits, and redacted architecture walkthrough material.
