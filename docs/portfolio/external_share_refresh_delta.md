The external share refresh delta records the transition from the stale ROE-17 share pack to the V2 share pack generated from ROE-21.

- previous share pack status: `stale`
- new share pack status: `refreshed_from_roe21`
- AgentSec feedback second-run executed status

- claim ceiling visibility
- generated V2 artifact paths

The delta is a freshness and provenance artifact. It is not certification, proof, legal/privacy/license/eval-clean conclusion, quality guarantee, product readiness, or action authorization.
