# Owner Consent Policy

Owner consent describes who is allowed to provide an artifact for local review.

Supported statuses:

- `self_owned`: the requester owns the artifact.
- `owner_provided`: an owner or maintainer provided the artifact for review.
- `sample_fixture`: the artifact is a sample fixture.
- `insufficient` or `unknown`: the pilot should hold until consent is clarified.

Consent permits only the stated local review mode. Consent does not authorize external mutation, posting, merge, deploy, upload, model training, credential use, certification, legal clearance, or product-readiness claims.
