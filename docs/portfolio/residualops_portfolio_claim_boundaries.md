# ResidualOps Portfolio Claim Boundaries

The unified portfolio may describe local/offline receipts, reviewer artifacts, ALLOW/HOLD/BLOCK findings, feedback memory, known limits, and next actions.

It must not claim:

- no security certification
- no code correctness proof
- no legal compliance claim
- no privacy compliance claim
- no license clearance
- no evaluation-cleanliness proof
- no quality guarantee
- no production readiness
- no merge authorization
- no deploy authorization
- no training authorization
- no purchase or external action authorization

Private ranking weights, thresholds, provider priors, anti-gaming rules, private negative controls, private probe seeds, and commercial routing policy remain non-public.
