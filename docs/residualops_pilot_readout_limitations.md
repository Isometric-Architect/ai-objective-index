# ResidualOps Pilot Readout Limitations

Pilot readout is operating memory, not proof.

It can summarize receipt count, HOLD/BLOCK signals, failure notes, and missing inputs. It cannot verify a capability, certify security, guarantee quality, prove product readiness, prove legal/privacy/license/evaluation status, provide purchasing advice, or authorize actions.

## Public/Private Boundary

Public artifacts may describe readout decisions and second-run HOLD reasons. They must not expose private ranking calibration values, threshold policy, provider priors, private negative controls, private probe seeds, partner strategy, private customer data, or commercial routing policy.
