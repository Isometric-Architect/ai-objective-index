# ROE Portfolio Strategy

ROE-0 keeps AOI alive as the public Objective-to-Capability Trust Router while preparing a parallel ResidualOps product family. The decision is to avoid opening three broad products at once.

Implementation order:

1. QIRA-Code ReleaseGate MVP
2. AgentSec Gate MVP
3. DataCapsule / AIDREG Engine MVP

Market priority is slightly different: AgentSec has the strongest first buyer signal, DataCapsule has the largest long-platform surface, and QIRA has the fastest implementation proof. QIRA is first because the QBCPL source pack already maps cleanly to behavior contracts, patch receipts, residual ledgers, and action-license boundaries.

Public ROE language may expose packet, probe, validator, residual, receipt, CertCost, d_raw/d_eco, and ALLOW/HOLD/BLOCK concepts at a high level. Ranking-weight values, thresholds, anti-gaming rules, provider priors, private negative-control banks, private probe seeds, and commercial routing policy remain private.

ROE-0 is not verification, security certification, a quality guarantee, product readiness, legal advice, purchasing advice, or external action authorization.
