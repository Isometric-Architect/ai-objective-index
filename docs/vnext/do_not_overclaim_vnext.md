# Do Not Overclaim vNext

Allowed:

- AI Agent Capability Trust Router strategy
- source-traced capability candidates
- objective-relative routing
- ALLOW / HOLD / BLOCK
- roadmap/schema upgrade

Not allowed:

- Do not claim AOI is already a security gateway.
- Do not claim AOI verifies capabilities.
- Do not claim AOI verifies MCP servers.
- Do not claim AOI security certifies tools.
- Do not claim AOI quality guarantees tools.
- Do not claim AOI is an official standard.
- Do not claim all agents will use AOI.
- Do not claim AOI provides purchasing advice.
- Do not claim AOI performs payment, booking, login, email, purchase, form submission, contract signing, account connection, supplier claim, supplier verification, or profile modification.

vNext is a strategy and schema alignment until implementation evidence exists.

- ALLOW means verified.

- CapabilityTrust is a security gateway.
