# ExecutionReceipt Limitations

ExecutionReceipt memory is useful, but narrow.

Limitations:

- self-reported receipts are not independent verification;
- local fixtures are not production validation;
- public issue reports may be incomplete;
- source traces can be stale or partial;
- receipt success does not establish safety, security, legal compliance, quality, product readiness, or purchasing advice;
- receipt memory does not authorize payment, booking, login, email, purchase, contract, supplier claim, or profile modification actions.

Use receipts to lower uncertainty, record failures, and guide follow-up review. Do not use them as certification.
