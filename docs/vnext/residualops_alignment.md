# ResidualOps Alignment

AOI vNext aligns with ResidualOps-style vocabulary without claiming to be the full executable ResidualOps Engine.

Mapping:

- `Q`: objective or query.
- `f`: local front readout from metadata, source traces, and missing fields.
- `q`: target route decision.
- `E-/E+`: negative and positive local evidence signals.
- residual channel: missing fields, source-trace gaps, policy uncertainty, and probe/receipt notes.
- receipt: ExecutionReceipt and ProbeReceipt local memory.
- `d_raw` / `d_eco`: cautious coverage and evidence-quality language, not public anti-gaming weights.
- route decision: ALLOW / HOLD / BLOCK.
- LicenseToken: not implemented as external action authorization; no external action authorization is granted.

AOI does not issue an external action license, does not authorize payment or account actions, and does not certify security, legal readiness, product readiness, or quality.
